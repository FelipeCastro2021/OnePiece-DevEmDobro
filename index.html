Tony Tony Chopper

This little reindeer gained the ability to change its shape and think like humans after eating the Hito Hito no Mi fruit. Chopper is a valued member of the Straw Hat Pirates crew, serving as the crew's doctor.

Roronoa Zoro

The first pirate (second member if we count Luffy) to join the Straw Hat Pirates crew, Zoro accepted Luffy's invitation after the captain saved his life.

Monkey D. Luffy

"Straw Hat Luffy", as he became known, is the protagonist of the anime, and the founder and captain of the Straw Hat Pirates crew. Since he was very young, his biggest dream is to one day find the legendary treasure of Gol D. Roger, to become the new King of Pirates.

Nami

A war orphan, as a child Nami was adopted by Bell-mère, a Navy woman. While growing up alongside her adoptive sister Nojiko, Nami already demonstrated her passion for drawing maps, dreaming of one day making a map of the entire world.

Sanji

Also known as "Black Leg" Sanji, this pirate was the fifth to join Luffy's crew. His actions as a pirate earned him the third highest bounty among Straw Hat members, in addition to serving as the group's official cook.

Nico Robin

Nico Robin, also known as "Demon Child" and "Light of Revolution", is the archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates. She is the only survivor of the destroyed island of Ohara, located in the West Blue. As a result, she is currently the only person in the world with the ability to read and decipher Poneglyphs, an ability that is considered forbidden and which threatens the World Government.

Brook

Brook is the Straw Hat Pirates' musician. He is a skeleton that the Straw Hats found aboard a ghost ship after entering the Florian Triangle. He is a Devil Fruit user who ate the Yomi Yomi no Mi, which makes him a pseudo-immortal because of his powers. He is the ninth member of the Straw Hat Gang, and the eighth to join Luffy's crew. He is also one of two Swordsmen aboard his current ship, the other being Zoro. He fills the role of the long-awaited musician that Luffy has wanted for his crew since his journey began.

Jinbe

Jinbe is a fish-man being a whale shark and a powerful master of Fish-Man karate. His dream is to fulfill his former captain Fisher Tiger's dying wish for coexistence and equality between humans and fishmen.

Franky

Franky is the carpenter of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is a 36-year-old cyborg from Water 7 and was introduced into the story as the leader of the Franky Family, a ship-breaking group.

Yamato

Yamato is the son of Kaidou of the beasts, a former Yonkou after being defeated by Monkey D. Luffy. Born as Kaidou's daughter, his deep admiration for the legendary samurai of the country of Wano, Kozuki Oden, led Yamato to embody everything related to him, impersonating him and adopting his mannerisms. As a result of this desire to become Oden, Yamato decided to become a man and began to identify himself as Kaidou's son.
